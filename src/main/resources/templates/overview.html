What is Green Computing?

Green computing, also known as sustainable computing, is the environmentally responsible and eco-friendly use of computers and their resources. It encompasses the design, manufacture, use, and disposal of computing devices to reduce their environmental impact.

Key Components

  • Sustainable software practices
  • Responsible e-waste management
